Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects may take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, we offer various support packages to ensure your digital solution continues to perform optimally. This includes technical support, updates, and optimization services tailored to your needs.
What is your revision policy?
We include a specific number of revision rounds in each package to ensure your complete satisfaction. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an hourly rate, which we discuss upfront.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and are experienced in managing projects across different time zones. Our communication process is designed to be flexible and accommodating.
